"Will it actually sound like me, or sound generic?"

This is why Author DNA and the Story Excavator exist. The AI doesn't write until it knows exactly who you are. Every chapter is written using your voice profile, your real personal stories, and your own teaching frameworks. Early users describe reading their chapters back and thinking someone had transcribed one of their sermons.
