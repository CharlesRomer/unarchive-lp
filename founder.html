Still Thinking?

Let's address the real questions.

"Am I cheating by using AI to write my book?"

Unarchive is not a ghostwriter. It is a scribe. You provide your tone, your stories, your frameworks... The AI formats your words into chapters. The Apostle Paul used a scribe to write his letters. That didn't make them less his.

"Will it actually sound like me, or sound generic?"

Author DNA and the Story Excavator exist for exactly this reason. The AI doesn't write a single word until it knows your cadence, your phrases, your analogies. Early users describe reading chapters back and thinking someone transcribed one of their teachings.

"What if I'm not technical enough to use this?"

No prompts to memorize. No settings to configure. A built-in guide named Archie walks you through every step on screen. If you can fill out a form and speak into a microphone, you can write your book. Built for leaders, not developers.

"What if I don't have existing notes or content?"

That's exactly why the Story Excavator exists. You don't need a Word document. The AI interviews you, pulling stories, frameworks, and insights from your decades of experience. Your life is the content.

"How is this different from ChatGPT?"

ChatGPT knows nothing about you. It writes generic text in a corporate voice and loses your context by chapter three. Unarchive builds your complete voice profile before writing a single word and generates every chapter with your entire book's context loaded simultaneously.
